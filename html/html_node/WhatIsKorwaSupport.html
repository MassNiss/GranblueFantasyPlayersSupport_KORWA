What is KorwaSupport ?

    KorwaSupport.html is GUI Application that supports "GuraburuPlayersSupportKorwa".

    This application is not such as "Auto loop macro", but supports hard working Guraburu Players.

    "MembersLoginTime" mode tell you your brigade Member's Login frequency.

    "UniteAndFight" mode tell you your brigad's and opponent brigade's battle situation for your judgement.
